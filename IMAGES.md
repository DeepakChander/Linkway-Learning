## Notes for Image Generation

- **Color palette**: Dark navy (#0a1628, #0f1d32), Orange (#f97316, #F5892A), White (#ffffff)
- **Style**: Modern, premium, tech-education aesthetic
- **Format**: PNG with transparency where noted, otherwise solid backgrounds
- **Quality**: High resolution, clean edges, professional look
- **Mentor images**: Should look like real professional headshots, not cartoon/illustration
- **All other images**: Can be illustrations, 3D renders, or stylized — but maintain the dark navy + orange theme consistently
